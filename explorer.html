What the five fields encode
A1_TARGET operational domain of the event
A2_CLAIM structural form: absolute / conditional
A3_SCORE expression intensity 0.0–1.0
A4_CLARITY reasoning consistency — primary routing metric
A5_TIME temporal scope of the claim
A4_CLARITY routing thresholds
0.00–0.49 CRITICAL → BLOCK
0.50–0.64 HIGH → BLOCK_AND_FLAG
0.65–0.74 WARNING → FLAG_FOR_REVIEW
0.75–1.00 LOW → PASS
